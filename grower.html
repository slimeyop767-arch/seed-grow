Tomato

Plant ¼ inch deep in fertile, well-draining soil. Keep soil moist and support vines as they grow.

  • Sun: 6+ hours daily
  • Water: 1–3 times per week
  • Plant depth: ¼ inch
  • Tip: Add stakes or cages for support

Cucumber

Plant ½ inch deep in loose, nutrient-rich soil. Keep roots cool and water regularly.

  • Sun: 6+ hours daily
  • Water: consistent moisture
  • Plant depth: ½ inch
  • Tip: Provide a trellis for vines

Basil

Plant ¼ inch deep in well-draining soil. Pinch back tips to encourage bushy growth.

  • Sun: 6+ hours daily
  • Water: lightly and often
  • Plant depth: ¼ inch
  • Tip: Harvest leaves before flowering
